Recommended Books on Indian Astronomy

Below is a selection of key texts, both historical and modern, that delve into various aspects of Indian astronomy.

1. Surya Siddhanta

An ancient Sanskrit treatise on astronomy, the Surya Siddhanta is one of the earliest known texts detailing timekeeping, planetary motions, and eclipses.

2. Aryabhatiya

Written by Aryabhata, this seminal work discusses a range of topics in mathematics and astronomy, including place-value notation, trigonometry, and planetary models.

3. Brahma Sphuta Siddhanta

Authored by Brahmagupta, it expanded on astronomical calculations, geometry, and introduced advanced concepts in algebra and zero.

4. Siddhanta Shiromani

Bhaskara II’s treatise is divided into four parts—Lilavati, Bijaganita, Ganitadhyaya, and Goladhyaya—covering arithmetic, algebra, and spherical astronomy.

5. Modern Studies on Indian Astronomy

Various modern publications explore the historical evolution and global impact of Indian astronomical ideas, bridging ancient wisdom and contemporary science.
