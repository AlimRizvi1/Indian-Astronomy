Notable Figures

Aryabhata (476–550 CE)

Aryabhata is known for his significant contributions to mathematics and astronomy, including accurate calculations of the Earth's circumference and solar system dynamics.

Varahamihira (505–587 CE)

Varahamihira made remarkable advances in astrology and astronomy, with works that influenced scientific thought for centuries.

Bhaskara II (1114–1185 CE)

Recognized for his comprehensive treatise Siddhanta Shiromani, Bhaskara II made lasting contributions to astronomical observations and mathematical techniques.
