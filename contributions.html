Major Contributions

Indian astronomers have influenced global understanding of the cosmos through:

  • Development of astronomical instruments
  • Innovative mathematical techniques for calculations
  • Advanced timekeeping and calendar systems
  • Early models of planetary motion

These achievements enriched astronomy and paved the way for future scientific discoveries.
