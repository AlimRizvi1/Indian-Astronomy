An Ancient Legacy

Indian astronomy has roots that extend back thousands of years. Texts like the Vedanga Jyotisha showcase early astronomical practices and timekeeping.

Over centuries, scholars refined observations and mathematical calculations, laying the foundation for modern astronomy.

The history of Indian astronomy is a rich tapestry that spans thousands of years, marked by significant contributions from ancient scholars, the development of sophisticated astronomical instruments, and the integration of astronomy with cultural and religious practices.
